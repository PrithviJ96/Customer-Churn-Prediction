# **Customer Churn Prediction Insights:**



##### **Exploratory Data Analysis:**



1. Customers with month-to-month contracts show the highest churn, suggesting a need to promote long-term plans and incentives.

2. High monthly charges are strongly linked to churn, indicating opportunities for targeted discounts or flexible pricing.

3. Customers with low tenure are more likely to leave, highlighting the importance of early engagement and onboarding strategies.

4. Users without value-added services (e.g., support, security) have higher churn rates, suggesting bundling services to improve retention.

5. Certain payment methods (e.g., electronic check) are associated with higher churn, indicating a need to encourage more stable payment options.

6. Customers with low engagement or infrequent usage are at higher risk, requiring personalized offers and re-engagement campaigns.
